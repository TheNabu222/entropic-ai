## Key Story Elements & Concepts

### The Bild Lili Doll
- **Historical significance**: Pre-Barbie German doll from the 1950s
- **In-game function**: Key artifact that unlocks time-travel/historical consciousness
- **Symbolism**: Represents the cycle of stereotypes placed on women
- **Location**: Rogers Park antique shop
- **Effect**: Creates "chronal distortion" and temporal loop when activated

### The Glenwood
- **Location**: Open mic venue in Rogers Park, Chicago (49th Ward)
- **Atmosphere**: "Ozone, desperation, burnt coffee"
- **Regulars**: Artists, eccentrics, philosophers, "weirdos"
- **Significance**: Nexus point where all characters converge
- **Events**: Open mics, Rizz Battle, political rallies

### Rogers Park / 49th Ward
- **Setting**: Chicago neighborhood
- **Political context**: Has an alderman position (becomes plot point)
- **Vibe**: Urban, diverse, artistic community
- **Key locations**: The Glenwood, antique shops

### The Rizz Battle
- **Format**: Wrestling ring setup at The Glenwood
- **Measurement**: "RIZZ-O-METER" device
- **Sypher's manipulation**: Recalibrates to measure "statistical anomalies in speech patterns, unexpected references, and moments of high social awkwardness"
- **Outcome**: Leads to political candidacies

### The Alderman Campaign
- **Position**: Alderman for
