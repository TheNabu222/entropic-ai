Square:
• Pluto challenges the outward projection and self-image: The square
aspect creates tension and challenges between Pluto's transformative
energy and the individual's outward projection. It prompts the
individual to confront and address conflicts in aligning their inner
changes with their external identity.
• Pluto creates friction with self-expression and self-discovery: This
aspect generates friction between Pluto's intensity and the
individual's self-expression and self-discovery. It can lead to moments
of internal conflict and self-doubt.
• Obstacles in integrating inner transformation with external
representation: The square aspect signifies obstacles and difficulties
in integrating inner transformation with self-representation.
• Tension between personal power and vulnerability in interactions: The
individual might experience inner tension as they grapple with finding
the right balance between asserting personal power and allowing
vulnerability in their interactions.

Sextile:

projection: The sextile aspect indicates favorable potential for

presents themselves to others.

Semi-Square:

semi-square aspect creates subtle conflicts between the individual's
inner transformation and their public persona.

external projection.
• Petty triggers caused by intensity and self-presentation: The

the intensity of their inner changes and how they present themselves to
others.
